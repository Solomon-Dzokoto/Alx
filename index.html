** This fee includes your access to the ALX ecosystem and Pathway Foundations. Partner university eligibility and associated fee requirements apply.

FAQs

What is ALX Pathways?

ALX Pathway is a revolutionary approach for high school graduates looking to take their next step toward a future in tech. As part of Pathway, participants gain access to opportunities to develop their entrepreneurial, leadership, and professional skills and can choose to accelerate their tech career with one of our university partners.

How do I access Partner Universities?

In order to access our partner universities, you must first join our future-first talent development ecosystem, and complete the 8-months of Pathway Foundations. Upon successful completion of Pathways, participants will have the ability to apply to university partners through their standard application and admissions process and must meet the requirements as outlined by each university partner. Scholarships and sponsorships are limited and not guaranteed.

Which Universities does ALX partner with?

ALX is proud to partner with leading universities, including ALU and Lincoln University. ALX will continue to add more global partners over the next several months, with more information to follow soon. If you would like to be kept updated on our partners, application timings, and further details about this incredible opportunity, register your interest now

How do I apply?

Early Applications are now open until 24th July 2024. Regular applications will open on 1 August 2024. To start your application, please click the apply now button at the top of the page. 

Do you have any pre-requisites?

There are no learning prerequisites for Pathway. Standard ALX eligibility requirements apply. You will need to ensure you are available and dedicated to spend up to 50 hours per week to complete the Pathways experience, and have access to a laptop or desktop computer with stable internet access. During the application process, we will assess your English proficiency, as well as logical reasoning skills.

What is the difference between Pathway Foundations and Professional Foundations?

Pathway Foundations equips you with the building blocks to support your future tech education and career aspirations. Over 8 months, you will refresh your logic, reasoning, and communication skills, gain entrepreneurial and professional insights, and prepare for your application to our partner universities with support from mentors.

Why do I need to participate in Pathway Foundations?

Over the 8 months of Pathway Foundations, we will prepare you with the essential experience to enable your next step in education and your future career. Pathway Foundations is the key to unlock access to future opportunities and prepares you for a successful application process and scholarship opportunities with our partner universities.

How much is the Access Fee?

For Early Applications until 24th July 2024, ALX is charging a $20 USD Access Fee. This fee can be paid in full or in monthly installments of $5 over 4 months. This access fee is available for early applications until 24th July 2024.

If I complete ALX Pathway Foundations, am I guaranteed acceptance into one of ALX’s University partner’s programmes?

No. University admission is NOT guaranteed. Applicants must meet the application and admission criteria according to each Partner University programme.
