Every Journey Starts With a Single Step

Designed for High School Graduates

Pathway is a revolutionary approach for high school graduates looking to take their next step toward a future in tech.

JOIN

ALX Pathway

Pathway provides access to opportunities to help you take the next step toward a successful career. To support your journey, you will have access to:

  • ALX peer community and professional network

  • Mentorship and career guidance events

  • State-of-the-art tech hubs

PREPARE

Pathway Foundation

Pathway Foundations prepares and equips you with the building blocks to help you stand out and support your successful application to our university partners and beyond. It includes a focus on:

  • CV and application preparation

  • University interview and communication skills

  • Duration 8 Months

  • Quantitative reasoning

  • Entrepreneurial & critical thinking

  • Leading self and others

ACCESS

Partner Universities

Benefit from a global university and funding partner network.

  • Access to our network of partner universities

  • Scholarships & financial aid

  • Special pricing

  • Other perks only available to the ALX community

Prepare for Partner Universities

During Pathway Foundations, you can access essential infrastructure and personal development tools to support your application to one of our partner universities.

Develop & Prepare Yourself for the Future

Focus on developing yourself personally and professionally with an emphasis on 8 key meta-skills. Then, apply these meta-skills as you write your application to the university partner of your choice with the help of ALX mentors, advisors, and peers.

Get the ALX Experience

Join the ALX Community and your Pathway peers at your nearest Tech Hub. Attend community events, and hear from industry experts, career advisors, and university reps to help you make the best decisions for your career.
